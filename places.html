COME AND CHECK TOP BEACHES IN THE PHILIPPINNES


BORACAY

One of the top islands in the Philippines, Boracay, is known for its beaches and has been blessed with a long length of soft white sand, beautiful, turquoise seas, and a breathtaking sunset.Boracay, has received several honors in past years.

CEBU

In the Visayas region, the city of Cebu is home to several beaches with fine, white sand and crystal-clear, azure seas. Other activities such as tanning, swimming, snorkeling, camping, and island hopping, you may also spend a lot of time here.
